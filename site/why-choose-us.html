Frequently Asked

Questions Our Partners Ask

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product category, typically starting from a single 20ft container. Contact our team with your specific product for exact MOQ details.

Which incoterms do you support?

We support FOB, CIF, CFR, EXW and DDP shipping terms, and can advise on the most cost-effective option for your route and volume.

How do you ensure product quality?

Every consignment passes through independent, third-party pre-shipment inspection and laboratory testing before dispatch, with certificates shared ahead of loading.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible terms including LC at sight, TT advance and negotiated credit terms for long-term partners, assessed on a case-by-case basis.

How long does a typical shipment take?

Transit times depend on destination and mode of shipping, typically ranging from 10-45 days by sea. Our team provides an estimated timeline with every quotation.
