Our Story

Founded on a Simple Belief

HK Worldwide Exim Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2007 on a simple belief: that global trade should be built on trust, not just transactions. What began as a small spice-trading desk has grown into a diversified export house spanning agricultural products, industrial materials, textiles and minerals.

Today, our team of 140+ trade professionals, quality inspectors and logistics experts works across three continents, ensuring every shipment — whether a single container or a bulk vessel — meets the exacting standards our partners have come to expect.

Our Mission

To deliver superior agricultural and industrial products to global markets through ethical sourcing, rigorous quality control and dependable logistics.

Our Vision

To be Asia's most trusted export partner — recognised globally for reliability, transparency and long-term relationships built on results.

Core Values

Integrity, quality without compromise, respect for every partner, and a culture of continuous improvement in everything we ship.
